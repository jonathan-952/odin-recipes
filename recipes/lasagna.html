Steps

  1. In a large skillet, brown the ground beef with chopped onion and minced garlic.
  2. Add crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, tomato sauce, water, sugar, basil, and salt to the skillet. Simmer for 30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  3. In a bowl, combine ricotta or cottage cheese with 2 cups of mozzarella and 1/2 cup Parmesan cheese.
  4. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  5. Spread a thin layer of the meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish.
  6. Place a layer of cooked lasagna noodles over the sauce.
  7. Spread a layer of the cheese mixture over the noodles.
  8. Repeat the layers, ending with a layer of meat sauce on top.
  9. Sprinkle the remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheese over the top.
  10. Bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes or until hot and bubbly.
  11. Let it stand for 15 minutes before cutting into squares.
  12. Sprinkle chopped fresh parsley on top before serving.
