Mac and Cheese

Description:

The food you look forward to the most for Thanksgiving.

Ingredients

  • cheese, whatever cheese you want tbh
  • pasta of course
  • butter
  • some type of heavy cream or milk or something like that idk
  • whatever other toppings you want like chick etc.

Steps

  1. Boil the macaroni: Cook the elbow macaroni until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. Make the cheese sauce: Melt butter, add flour, gradually whisk in milk until thickened.
  3. Season the sauce: Add salt, black pepper, paprika, and mustard powder to taste.
  4. Add cheese: Stir in shredded cheddar cheese until melted and smooth.
  5. Combine macaroni and cheese sauce: Mix the cooked macaroni into the cheese sauce.
  6. Serve: Spoon into bowls, optionally sprinkle with additional cheese.
  7. Optional baking (for a baked version): Preheat oven, transfer to a baking dish, bake until golden and bubbly.
  8. Garnish and enjoy: Sprinkle with paprika, parsley, or breadcrumbs if desired. Serve and enjoy!
