Pancakes

Description:

My favorite and for a good reason

Ingredients

  • milk, or water if you are disgusting
  • eggs
  • pancake mix or if you are him, make the mix yourself, i'm not listing that out though
  • vanilla extract
  • cinnamon
  • any other toppings

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the buttermilk, milk, egg, and melted butter.
  3. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and gently stir until just combined. Do not overmix; lumps are okay.
  4. Preheat a griddle or non-stick pan over medium heat and lightly grease with butter or cooking spray.
  5. For each pancake, pour 1/4 cup of batter onto the griddle.
  6. Cook until bubbles form on the surface of the pancake, then flip and cook the other side until golden brown.
  7. Repeat until all the batter is used, adjusting heat as needed.
  8. Serve the pancakes warm with your favorite toppings, such as maple syrup, fresh fruits, or whipped cream.
